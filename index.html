The Story

An oath broke the moon. Now the moon wants it back.

Long ago, the Vale was guarded by nine lunar houses. Their power came from a living moon-engine buried beneath the capital. When the houses betrayed their sacred oath, the engine fractured, flooding the world with luminous corruption.

You play as Kael Veyra, a relic-bound wanderer carrying the last clean spark of the old moon. Every district you cleanse reveals another lie, another ghost, and another piece of the truth behind the Neon Oath.
